I was captivated by the caramel aroma of the milk tea. It's no exaggeration to say that it tastes exactly like bubble tea, which I drink from Tenren or Kung Fu Tea.

Before the Okinawa Brown Sugar Puer Milk Tea, there was another product called Oolong Milk Tea. I have no idea why they produced Oolong Milk Tea before. The milk tastes burnt. Leaves a lasting impression that milk tea is awful.

But now, everything has gone. Everyone should try the Supreme Okinawa Brown Sugar Puer Milk Tea, a delectable drink.
